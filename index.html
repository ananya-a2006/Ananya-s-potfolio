Breast Cancer Classification

Problem: Predict whether a tumor is malignant or benign.

Dataset: Breast Cancer Wisconsin dataset.

Approach: Cleaned columns, encoded diagnosis, split data, trained SVM kernels and KNN.

Model used: SVM with RBF kernel and KNN.

Result: Best accuracy: 98.2%.

Key learning: Preprocessing and model selection strongly affect performance.
